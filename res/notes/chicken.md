# chicken

In addition to being delicious, chicken is extremely easy to make, nutritious, and can be part of a [[weight]] loss diet if you go easy on the oil.

## Copying Fast Food Recipes

Chicken restaurants are pretty good at making chicken (believe it or not), so I like to look up copycat recipes online and pick and choose ingredients and techniques.
